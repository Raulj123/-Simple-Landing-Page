About Zymo Research

Zymo Research is a leading provider of innovative sample collection reagents, kits, and devices in the biomedical field. Since 1994, our mission has been to have a positive impact on biomedical science and contribute to the betterment of humanity. Our products have been used worldwide, including by NASA to protect biological samples in space. Recently, our DNA/RNA Shield received 510(k) clearance as a transport medium for COVID-19 testing and variant sequencing service. Upgrade the quality and preservation of your samples with our advanced sample collection kits, devices, and reagents, and start your journey toward impactful scientific discoveries.
